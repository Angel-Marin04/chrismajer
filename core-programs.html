EMBRACING DISRUPTION: NAVIGATING THE NEW WORLD OF BUSINESS

This transformative program is strategically designed to enhance employee engagement, boost morale, and fortify your organization. The end goal being the fostering of a resilient workforce culture as what we know is that work isn't working right now!

During this session, participants will acquire invaluable insights and tools to address the paramount challenge facing businesses today, which can best be summed up as attempting to manage a modern-day work force with industrial era management practices.

Explore the historically unprecedented shifts across all sectors of the economy. Changes which have seen dramatic movement in every sector of the economy except one – Management! Delve into the reasons why the existing work dynamics are no longer effective. It's much bigger than the remote work vs office work tug of war. See the results of in-depth customer research, competitive analysis, and creative value propositions so you can refine messaging strategies, and empower your team to elevate their skills to compete at higher levels.

Accelerate the demand for and pace of continuous learning so you can ride the wave of this emerging new world order instead of getting washed away by it.

YOUR PARTICIPANTS WILL LEARN:
  • The impact of the Coordination Era: Understand the significant shift in the drivers of the economy and how it has transformed the skills required for success from production-based work to effective coordination, which is communication, collaboration, cooperation, and mobilization.
  • The limitations of industrial-era management: Identify the challenges and shortcomings of managing modern coordination-era workers using outdated management thinking and practices.
  • Strategies for building engagement: Discover practical techniques for fostering employee engagement and motivation in the coordination era, improving overall team performance and success.
  • Adapting management thinking and practices: Learn how to align management approaches with the needs and realities of the modern workforce, enabling effective communication, collaboration, and coordination.
  • Creating a new future: Gain actionable insights and tools to create a future-focused organization that thrives in the Coordination Era, unlocking new possibilities for success and competitiveness.
